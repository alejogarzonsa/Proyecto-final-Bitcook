Boyancese-tamal

The Boyacense tamale differs from other Colombian tamales by using corn flour. Also, among its characteristics, is that rijua or chisgua leaves are used for the wrapping. These variations may be the secret of its flavor.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 76 minutes.
  • Preparation: 36 minutes.
  • Cooking: 40 minutes.

Instructions

  1. In a deep container, add the corn flour, the Maggi chicken broth envelope and the water, mix until well integrated and the dough is lump-free, add the peas, stir again, cover and set aside until ready to use.
  2. In a frying pan, heat the oil over medium heat, add the onion, tomato, maggi rib broth envelope, water, cilantro and cook for 5 minutes or until the hogao thickens a little.
  3. Cut the banana leaves into large squares and put 2 leaves per tamale, in the center of the leaf put a little of the dough, a little hogao, the meats, a slice of potato, one of egg and one of carrot.
  4. Close the tamale with the first leaf and reinforce with the second, making a tying shape with a little pita and tie tightly, until you finish with all the dough and all the ingredients for the tamales.
  5. Place a large pot over medium heat, place pieces of remaining banana leaves and the 3 liters of water on the bottom, place the tamales on top and cook for approximately 30 to 40 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and serve hot.
  7. Let the leftover tamales cool very well and store them in the refrigerator to consume later.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound yellow corn (500 g)
  • 1 sachet of SHREDDED MAGGI® CHICKEN BROTH (9 g)
  • 1 pound of precooked green peas (500 g)
  • 2 tablespoons of oil (28 g)
  • 4 tomatoes, peeled and finely chopped (492 g)
  • 1 sachet of maggi rib broth (9 g)
  • 4 stalks of long onion including the green part finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of water (250 ml)
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped cilantro (20 g)
  • 2 pounds cooked pork ribs, cut into 8 pieces (1000 g)
  • 2 pounds of pork cut into 8 pieces and previously cooked (1000 g)
  • 1 1/2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cut into slices (750 g)
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and cut into slices (140 g)
  • 4 hard-boiled eggs (200 g)
